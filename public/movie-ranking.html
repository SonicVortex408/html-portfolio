The Best Movies According to Riya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

It's really moving to see the father-daughter bond evolve in the themes of space and time, crisis and evolution. The greatest sci-fi movie of all-time.

Kill Bill

Beatrix kiddo is my favorite lady heros of hollywood. Uma Thurman nailed the Hattori Hanzo training. Loved all the gory fight scenes and watch her fight with cotton mouth wearing a kimono!

Kung Fu Panda

It's a bowl of warm chicken soup to my inner child. Love watching this movie not just because Po actually is such a good cook and makes some mout-watering noodle soup bowls but also because of Master Oogway's wise teachings, to watch Po believe in himself and that there is no secret ingredient, it's just you..
